About

Chelsea-based DJ and producer Max de Luz is known for creating immersive musical journeys that bring atmosphere, energy and flow to an evening.

For Max, a great set is more than a collection of songs. It has a sense of direction. It evolves naturally throughout the evening, responding to the energy of the room and guiding the atmosphere from one moment to the next. The result is a musical experience that feels seamless, considered and perfectly attuned to the occasion.

With a musical background that includes Berklee College of Music, Max brings an international perspective to every event. From summer seasons in Punta del Este and performances in Ibiza to intimate celebrations and members’ clubs in London, his approach remains the same: thoughtful and deeply attuned to the atmosphere in the room.

Over the years, Max has become an established presence within the Chelsea and West London events scene. As a resident DJ at one of the area’s longstanding venues, he has developed a reputation for his distinctive musical selections, balancing the familiar with the unexpected to create evenings that feel fresh, engaging and impossible to predict.

Alongside his work as a DJ and producer, Max’s background in live sound engineering provides a deeper understanding of the technical and logistical elements that allow an event to unfold seamlessly. He sees his role as one of curating the experience as a whole.
